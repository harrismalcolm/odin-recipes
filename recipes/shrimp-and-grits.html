Cheesy Shrimp and Grits

Description

This recipe for cheesy shrimp and grits is an easy, creamy, flavorful classic Southern dish loaded with gooey cheese, tender shrimp, and crispy bacon. It's sure to become a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup stone-ground grits

  • 2 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese

  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter 

  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

  • 6 strips bacon, chopped

  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined

  • 1 cup thinly sliced scallions

  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

  • 4 teaspoons lemon juice

  • 1 large clove garlic, minced

Steps

  1. Cook grits until tender according to package directions. Off heat, stir in Cheddar cheese and butter; season with salt and black pepper. Cover and set aside.
  2. Cook bacon in a large skillet over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes. Drain on paper towels, leaving drippings in the skillet.
  3. Meanwhile, rinse shrimp; pat dry.
  4. Cook shrimp in drippings in the same skillet over medium heat until begin to turn opaque, about 2 minutes. Add bacon, scallions, parsley, lemon juice, and garlic; continue cooking until shrimp are bright pink, about 3 minutes more.
  5. Spoon grits into a serving bowl. Add shrimp mixture; mix well. Serve immediately.
